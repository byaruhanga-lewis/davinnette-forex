About Davinnette
Forex Bureau

Davinnette Forex Bureau Limited is a privately owned limited liability company,
incorporated in Uganda, licensed and regulated
by Bank of Uganda offering foreign exchange,
money remittance, agency banking and mobile money.

Davinnette Forex Bureau is currently located in Kampala central at Grand imperial building
shop 4 directly opposite Bank of Uganda.

Davinnette Forex Bureau was registered on 23rd -June - 1996 with the Uganda Registration Services Bureau and started operating on 23rd – August – 1996.

Davinnette Vision is to be a world-class Institution with reliable, professional, and efficient service delivery.

With the mission of providing innovative solutions to all financial needs by earning people’s trust in the most friendly, professional, and efficient manner possible.

AML Policy

Davinnette Forex Bureau is committed to the highest standards of Anti Money Laundering (AML) and Anti – Terrorist Financing and ensures that employees follow the required standards.

We critically examine our AML objectives and maintain an effective AML policy that reflects the best compliance and best practices as a Financial Institution

Davinnette AML policy includes efficient KYC, and Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD), following the legal and regulatory requirements provided by the regulators[n1].
