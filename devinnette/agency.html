Agency Banking

This is a type of branchless banking that allows banks to extend their network of branches and services in a cost efficient manner through authorised agents.

Various banks work with Dhahab International to deliver financial services to un-banked customers. In such a way our customers gain convenient financial services like cash withdrawals, cash deposits, tax payments, bill payments, among others in their own communities.
